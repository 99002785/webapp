About - ManGo

Hotel Maan-Go is spread across a large 8 acre property with uniquely carved and textured exterior that exhibit a reflection of the influence of traditional temple architectures, with particular reference to the Brihadeeshwara, Gangaikondaicholapuram and Airavateshwara temples.
